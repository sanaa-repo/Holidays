Holidays
Christmas

Traditionally, Christmas has been an annual holiday celebrating the birth of Christ. Since then, it has also evolved into a cultural holiday characterized by exchanging presents, decorating Evergreen trees, and Santa Claus. It takes place on December 25th every year.

Gingerbread houses originated in Germany after the publication of "Hansel and Gretel."

In 2020, total holiday retail sales peaked at $789.4 billion.

According to a 2018 international Exeter Santa Survey, close to 85% of American kids believe in Santa Claus.
